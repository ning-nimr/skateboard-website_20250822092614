What is Skateboarding?

Skateboarding is an action sport that involves riding and performing tricks using a skateboard. It's a recreational activity, an art form, a job, and a method of transportation. Skateboarding has been shaped and influenced by many skateboarders throughout the years. A 2009 report found that the sport had 11.08 million active skateboarders in the world. In 2016, it was announced that skateboarding would be represented at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan.
